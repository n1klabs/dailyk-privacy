Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Dailyk ("we", "our", or "the app") is a task management application for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address - for authentication and account recovery
  • Display name - to personalize your experience
  • User ID - a unique identifier for your account

You can sign in with email/password or Sign in with Apple. When using Sign in with Apple, we receive only the data Apple provides based on your preferences.

Location Data

If you enable location-based reminders, the app accesses your precise location to:

  • Display your location on the map when selecting a task location
  • Monitor geofence regions to send reminders when you arrive at or leave a specified area

Location data is processed on your device and is not sent to our servers. Background location access is used solely for geofence monitoring.

Task Data

Your tasks, deadlines, notes, and other content you create are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is not transmitted to external servers.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Authentication: To create and secure your account
  • Location reminders: To notify you about tasks when you are near a specified location
  • App functionality: To provide core task management features

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google) - for user authentication. Firebase processes your email, name, and user ID. Firebase's privacy policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Task data is stored locally on your device only
  • Authentication data is managed by Firebase (hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure)
  • Location data is processed on-device and not stored externally
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your account - through the app's Settings. This removes your authentication data from Firebase
  • Revoke location access - through iOS Settings at any time
  • Delete task data - by deleting individual tasks or removing the app from your device

6. Children's Privacy

Dailyk is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
